What I Do

As a Network Security Engineer, I specialize in protecting and securing enterprise networks. My role involves implementing robust security measures to safeguard sensitive data and ensure the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of network resources.

  • 01

    Network Security

    I secure computer networks using firewalls, IDS/IPS, VPNs, and encryption, ensuring data protection and network integrity through strong authentication, access controls, and continuous monitoring.

  • 02

    CyberSecurity

    I safeguard digital assets by implementing robust security measures, including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS), and encryption protocols. I manage secure access through multi-factor authentication and stringent access controls. I ensure system reliability and scalability through continuous integration, continuous delivery, and proactive monitoring.
